Happy News DAO is powered by a decentralized application stack that was DAOveloped and consists of various technologies and platforms listed below:


Deform App

We use Deform for the journalist applications and to submit Fresh Articles for publication as well. You can follow Deform on twitter to learn more about them.




Our Article Storage

We use Filecoin to store our news articles in a decentralized manner. You can follow Filecoin on twitter to learn more about them.




Our App Deployment

We use Heroku to deploy our decentralized application stack. You can follow Heroku on twitter to learn more about them.




Getting Paid on Fantom

Our payment stream is built on Fantom because of their security, fast finality, and industry-leading transaction fees which enables secure and transparent financial payments to journalists on an hourly basis. You can follow the Fantom Foundation on twitter to learn more about them.




Hourly Payment Stream

To increase security, we use a feature built by Provenance Financial called MainStream that allows for a constant stream of payments to the same wallet address for as long as their articles are being clicked on and read on twitter. You can follow Heroku on twitter to learn more about them.




Superfluid Fork

Provenance Financial forked the MainStream feature above from the Superfluid repository and deployed it to Fantom where it is free to use for anyone. You can follow Superfluid on twitter to learn more about them.




Our Wallet + Website

Our wallet & website at HappyNews.DAO is a domain from Unstoppable Domains and is deployed on the Polygon Blockchain. The documents for our website are stored on the IPFS as well. You can follow Unstoppable Domains on their twitter to learn more about them.




And of course, we were DAOveloped

Our entire dApp was DAOveloped from github to deployment, to the audit, and to the continuous maintence of Happy News DAO. You can follow DAOveloped on their twitter to learn more about them.
